This Power BI dashboard provides a comprehensive analysis of digital marketing campaigns across Facebook and Instgram . The project focuses on measuring user engagement, tracking conversions, and evaluating budget efficiency to help marketing teams optimize their digital presence.

Key Dimensions & Features

Performance KPI Cards: A snapshot of total reach and conversion metrics, including Total Likes, Comments, Clicks, Shares, Purchases, and Budget.

Platform Performance: A comparative analysis of Purchases by Platform (Facebook, Instagram, ) to determine which channel drive the highest ROI.

Audience Demographics: Insights into Purchases by Gender and Age Group, allowing for better customer profiling and targeted advertising.

Temporal Analysis: Identification of peak conversion periods through Purchases by Day times, helping to optimize ad scheduling.

Interactive Filters: Integrated Dynamic Slicers for Platforms, Dates, and Campaigns to allow for deep-dive exploration of the data.

Technical Implementation
Data Cleaning (Power Query): Processed raw campaign data, handled missing values, and standardized naming conventions for platforms and metrics.

Data Modeling: Established a star-schema inspired model to ensure fast and accurate filtering across multiple marketing dimensions.

Calculations (DAX): Created custom measures to calculate key metrics such as: Total Spend vs. Total Revenue.
